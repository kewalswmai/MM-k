Our Services

Rishabh Refrigeration Service, Inc. has been in the business of cold since 2016. We provide innovative solutions and the highest quality workmanship for all your refrigeration and process piping needs. Our areas of expertise include engineering, installation, service and maintenance. Our technicians can perform installation. Once the installation of your system is complete, our highly-capable team of Service Technicians can provide service and maintenance tailored to your particular needs. We help our customers freeze everything.

Installation

Rishabh Refrigeration Service utilizes highly trained pipe fitters and certified pipe welders. These experienced professionals have fabricated numerous refrigeration systems and take great pride in providing superior workmanship while meeting or exceeding all refrigeration codes and standards. Materials procured for fabrication are of the highest quality to assure you have a long lasting dependable system.

Service and Maintainance

Rishabh Refrigeration Service employs expert Service Technicians with years of experience in the ammonia refrigeration industry. All of our Technicians are knowledgeable in electrical, mechanical, and computer controls. We offer emergency service along with inspection and maintenance programs designed specifically for each customer, mechanical integrity inspections, commissioning of new equipment, compressor and pump rebuilds, control, trouble shooting and emergency shutdowns, to name a few.
